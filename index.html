Our ML model instantly finds the best-fit projects or freelancers using:

Project Kickoff & Escrow Setup

Once matched, a smart contract handles everything:

  • Client has 72 hours to approve or request edits
  • Smart contract auto-releases payment from escrow
  • Disputes handled by decentralized arbitration if needed
